Faculty of Education

The mission of the faculty is unique learning for those interested in inspiring young minds by incorporating the principles of true redemption education into the curriculum and providing excellent programs that prepare them to become professional teachers. Is to provide an opportunity. You can inspire students to become lifelong learners, guided by lifelong learning and research, and sound principles. The numerous programs offered by the USAL's Faculty of Education were created to prepare and qualify undergraduates as well as graduates seeking to enter the teaching profession according to the distinct quality requirements demanded by the labor market. Our program's objectives, competences, and results were founded on basic educational knowledge and trends, as well as the skills and international criteria that a teacher must meet in order to practice his or her profession. The department's goal is to assist instructors in gaining field experience directly from the most prominent Lebanese schools, where students have the opportunity to be educated as a program requirement at these institutions under the supervision of academic specialists.
