About Us

USAL is a welcoming private, independent academic institution of higher education.

It is licensed under decree # 1738, April 14, 2009. It started its operations in September 2013. The university academic degrees were validated by the Ministry of Higher Education in July 2015. Our mission is to integrate technology in the Lebanese curriculum to make educational resources and opportunities accessible for all students without any discrimination.
Our vision is to transform the educational experience in Lebanon and the region by providing equal, reliable, and quality resources for all students.
We Value to make learning fun, empower the youth , personalize resources ,offer equal opportunities and resources and to give back to the community."We Believe that Quality Education Should be Provided for All". 1. The Faculty of Management at the University of Sciences and Arts in Lebanon (USAL) is seeking to appoint instructors in the below fields (full-time or part-time positions): Business Communication Skills (business background is a must) Digital Analytics and Digital Marketing.Candidates in other business fields (Accounting, Finance, Hospitality Management, Human Resources Management, Management, Management Information systems, and Supply Chain Management) are strongly encouraged to apply for full-time (PhD holders) positions.
The Computer Science department at the University of Sciences and Arts in Lebanon (USAL) is seeking to appoint Full time and part time lecturers in the fields of Computer Science and Data Science.
The ideal candidate holds a PhD degree in his respective field from a reputable university with a significant research / market and teaching experience.
The Lebanese University of Sciences and Arts (USAL) strives for excellence in learning, teaching and community service. Since the first day of its launch, it has placed at the forefront of its interests the graduation of qualified students who are qualified to build a conscious and renewed missionary society. A society whose children carry in their intellectual inventory the values ​​of responsibility, dialogue, citizenship, acceptance of others and the skill of effective social communication. A modern educational society that establishes promising, educated generations that depend on objectivity in research, and originality in thought in the horizons of excellence, creativity and innovation, a balanced administrative society capable of addressing various gaps in systems and institutions, a developed technological society that keeps pace with the new, and competes with the most advanced countries with its limited capabilities, an open society that transmits its media The truth is pure objectively, transparently and responsibly. The university, with all its academic and applied departments, seeks to serve its community through pioneering scientific projects and various technical activities in cooperation with various loyal and capable events at home and abroad. At the same time, she attaches great importance to producing academic research and publishing it in international refereed scientific journals specializing in economics, education, technology and media, despite her recent experience and her young age.
